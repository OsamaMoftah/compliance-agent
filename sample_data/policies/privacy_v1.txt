# Sample Policy Document v1 — Company Data Protection Policy

## 1. Data Collection
We collect personal data including name, email address, and job title for the purpose of account registration and service delivery. All data collection is subject to explicit user consent.

## 2. Data Processing
Personal data is processed on servers located within the European Union. We do not transfer personal data outside the EU without adequate safeguards.

## 3. Data Retention
Personal data is retained for the duration of the user's account plus 12 months. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

## 4. Automated Decision Making
Our platform does not use automated profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal effects concerning data subjects.

## 5. Data Security
We implement encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security audits to protect personal data.

## 6. User Rights
Users have the right to access, rectify, and delete their personal data. Requests are processed within 30 calendar days.

## 7. Third-Party Sharing
Personal data is shared with third-party service providers only for purposes of service delivery and under strict data processing agreements.

## 8. Consent Management
Consent is obtained through clear, affirmative action. Users may withdraw consent at any time through account settings.
